Delete your Orvia account and data

You can permanently delete your Orvia account and everything tied to it, at any time. Here's exactly what happens and how to do it.

01 Delete from inside the app

  1. 1Open Settings, then tap your profile at the top to open Account.
  2. 2Scroll down and tap Delete account. Confirm your identity, then confirm the deletion.

02 What happens to your data

Permanently removed

  • Profile & onboarding details
  • Tasks, goals & milestones
  • Focus sessions & activity history
  • AI assistant conversations
  • Feedback you've sent

Kept on record

  • Purchase & subscription records
  • Retained only for financial and legal recordkeeping
  • Not used to identify you after deletion

Deleting your account does not cancel an active Google Play subscription. Manage or cancel that separately in the Google Play Store.

03 Can't open the app?

If you've already removed Orvia from your device, email us from the address on your account and we'll process your deletion request.
